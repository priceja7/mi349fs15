Music

Experience

One day when it was rainy and I was bored back in high school, I went downstairs and found an old keyboard laying around. I plugged it in and began hitting random keys on it, thinking to myself that I should sit down and learn this instrument. So I began to search on the web 'how to play piano'. After some searching, I found a YouTube video for a song that I really liked. The video was a tutorial of how to play the song. It took me a few weeks to learn it, but once I did, I fell in love with playing. I began to search how to play more songs and learn them as well.

I've been doing this for probably about 4-5 years now, and I have no plans on stoping. I have taught myself music theory, scales, and I can now play music by ear (playing a song out of memory, just by hearing it).

Future

My future plans regarding music, include setting up a recording studio and begin to write original music. I would also like to expand my musical horizons to other instruments as well as vocals. After college, I plan to take vocal lessons in order to progress as a musical artist.
